There six default breakpoints in bootstrap

Breakpoints points                  Classes

(1) Extra small: <576px             None    
(2) small: 	≥576px                  sm
(3) Medium: ≥768px                  md
(4) Large: 	≥992px                  lg
(5) Extra large: ≥1200px            xl
(6) Extra extra large: ≥1400px      xxl


New In Bootstrap 5 

(6) Extra extra large: ≥1400px      xxl
