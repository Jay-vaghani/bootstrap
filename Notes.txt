=>  You can define different breakpoints on col to determine size of call in different screen size

There six default breakpoints in bootstrap like ve saw in previous part and in col you can use those breakpoints

Breakpoints points                              Classes

(1) Extra small: <576px                         None    
(2) small: 	≥576px to 768px                     sm
(3) Medium: ≥768px to 992px                     md
(4) Large: 	≥992px to 1200px                    lg
(5) Extra large: ≥1200px to 1400px              xl
(6) Extra extra large: ≥1400px                  xxl


New In Bootstrap 5 

(6) Extra extra large: ≥1400px      xxl
