=>  You can also use row-col class to define each and every col size inside that row
the row-col range is row-col-1 to row-col-12 


=>  you can also use six different bootstrap breakpoints in row-col class to adjust       different width of the col in row 

Breakpoints points                              Classes

(1) Extra small: <576px                         None    
(2) small: 	≥576px to 768px                     sm
(3) Medium: ≥768px to 992px                     md
(4) Large: 	≥992px to 1200px                    lg
(5) Extra large: ≥1200px to 1400px              xl
(6) Extra extra large: ≥1400px                  xxl


New In Bootstrap 5
